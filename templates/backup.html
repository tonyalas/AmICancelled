Am I Cancelled?


Get Started:

What is Am I Cancelled?

Am I Cancelled? is a Twitter App that searches through a large bank of words that may have been socially acceptable
years ago, but no longer have a place in today's society. It goes through your entire Twitter account history searching for
these terms in the word bank.

Why Did I Make This?

Am I Cancelled? is a Twitter App that is meant to help those who have forgotten about old tweets
they have made in the past that no longer accurately reflect their beliefs anymore.
I can only hope that this is used by those who have genuinely changed from their past selves.
