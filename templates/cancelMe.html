What is Am I Cancelled?

Am I Cancelled? is a Twitter App that searches through a large bank of words that may be cancel-worthy.
It goes through your entire Twitter account history searching for
these terms in the word bank.

Have Word Suggestions?

If you wish to know the list of words used or have any words that may not be included in the search,
please email me using the email link in the footer of this page.


* Disclaimers *

Due to the nature of the Twitter Search API, accounts that are private will not be able to use this.
I do not collect or store any of your information. All of the authentication is done by the Twitter Standard.
This is my first official project, so it may not be perfect. Please contact me if there any major bugs.
Lastly, I hope you enjoy it. :)
